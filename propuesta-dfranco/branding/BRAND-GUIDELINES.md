## Logo Treatment

The D'Franco wordmark uses **Cormorant Garamond** with:
- The **D'** in *gold italic* — distinctive accent
- **Franco** in *white/cream* — clean and elegant
- No icon — the ornate script reads as its own logo

For dark backgrounds: Gold D' + White Franco  
For light backgrounds: Gold D' + Charcoal Franco  

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Sophisticated** but **welcoming** — luxury salon energy, neighborhood warmth
- **Bilingual-friendly** — English-first with Spanish touchpoints (tagline, key phrases)
- **Confident** — "Master Colorist," "Full Service," "Your crown"
- **Visual-first** — Let the work speak; gorgeous transformations front and center

---

## Photography Direction

- **Before & After** shots of color transformations (the salon's strongest selling point)
- **Warm, natural lighting** — avoid harsh flash; embrace golden-hour warmth
- **Texture focus** — close-ups of hair shine, dimension, movement
- **Salon atmosphere** — inviting shots of the interior, mirrors, styling stations
- **Team in action** — candid shots working on clients
